# 2024 Employee Benefits Guide

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Plan Year:** Jan 1 – Dec 31
**Benefits Admin:** benefits@company.com

## Health Insurance Options
The company pays **85% of premiums** for employees and **60% of premiums** for dependents.

### Option A: High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP) - *Aetna Choice POS II*
* **Best for:** Healthy individuals who want lower monthly costs and HSA access.
* **Employee Monthly Cost:** $45.00
* **Deductible:** $3,000 (Individual) / $6,000 (Family)
* **Co-insurance:** 90% coverage after deductible.
* **HSA Eligible:** Yes.

### Option B: PPO Plan - *Aetna Select*
* **Best for:** Employees expecting frequent doctor visits or prescriptions.
* **Employee Monthly Cost:** $160.00
* **Deductible:** $750 (Individual) / $1,500 (Family)
* **Co-pay:** $25 Primary Care / $45 Specialist.
* **HSA Eligible:** No.

## Retirement: 401(k)
**Provider:** Fidelity
**Eligibility:** Immediate upon hire.

### Company Match
We offer a "Safe Harbor" match to help you save:
* **100% match** on the first **3%** of your salary contributed.
* **50% match** on the next **2%** of your salary contributed.
* *Example:* If you contribute 5%, we contribute 4%.

### Vesting
* **Employee Contributions:** 100% vested immediately.
* **Company Match:** 100% vested immediately (Safe Harbor provision).

## Ancillary Benefits

### Dental (Delta Dental)
* **Premium:** 100% Company Paid for Employee.
* **Preventive:** 100% covered (2 cleanings/year).
* **Basic/Major:** 80% / 50% coverage.
* **Annual Max:** $2,000 per person.

### Vision (VSP)
* **Premium:** $8.00/month.
* **Exam:** $10 co-pay once every 12 months.
* **Frames/Contacts:** $150 allowance every 12 months.

## Perks and Stipends

### Wellness Stipend
* **Amount:** **$75.00 per month**.
* **Eligible Expenses:** Gym memberships, yoga classes, meditation apps (Headspace/Calm), or home fitness equipment.
* **Reimbursement:** Submit via Expensify under category "HR - Wellness."

### Commuter Benefits
* **Pre-Tax Transit:** Up to $300/month deducted pre-tax.
* **Company Subsidy:** We provide a **$50/month subsidy** for public transit users (Clipper/MetroCard).

### Parental Leave
* **Primary Caregiver:** 16 weeks fully paid.
* **Secondary Caregiver:** 8 weeks fully paid.
* **Eligibility:** Must be employed for 6 months prior to the birth/adoption event.

## Enrollment Windows
1.  **New Hire:** You have **30 days** from your start date to enroll in Workday. Coverage is retroactive to Day 1.
2.  **Open Enrollment:** Occurs annually **November 1 – November 15**. Changes take effect Jan 1.
3.  **Qualifying Life Event:** Marriage, birth of child, or loss of spousal coverage. You must notify HR within **30 days** of the event.

## FAQ
**Q: What is the HSA contribution limit for 2024?**
A: $4,150 for individuals and $8,300 for families. The company contributes $500/year to your HSA if you choose the HDHP.

**Q: Do I get life insurance?**
A: Yes, we provide Basic Life AD&D equal to **1x your annual salary** at no cost to you. You may purchase up to 5x salary in supplemental coverage.
