# New Hire Onboarding Process

**Owner:** People Operations
**Contact:** onboarding@company.com

## Overview
This document outlines the mandatory steps for onboarding new employees. The standard onboarding period is considered the first **90 days** of employment.

## Phase 1: Pre-Boarding (Signed Offer to Day 1)
Once the offer letter is signed, the following triggers automatically:

1.  **IT Provisioning (10 Days before start):**
    * Laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS) shipped to home address.
    * Okta account created.
2.  **HR Paperwork (5 Days before start):**
    * New hire receives email from **Workday** to complete I-9 Section 1 and Direct Deposit info.
    * Background check initiated via **Checkr**.
3.  **Welcome Email:** Manager must send a welcome email with the Day 1 schedule 48 hours prior to start.

## Phase 2: Day 1 (Orientation)
* **9:00 AM:** IT Setup Zoom call (Password resets, Slack setup).
* **11:00 AM:** HR Orientation (Benefits overview, Handbook acknowledgment).
* **1:00 PM:** Team Lunch (Virtual or In-Person). Budget: $25/person.
* **3:00 PM:** Manager 1:1 (Role expectations, 30-60-90 day goal setting).

## Phase 3: Week 1 (Integration)
* **Compliance Training:** Complete standard courses in **LMS** (Harassment Prevention, Data Security, Phishing). **Deadline: Friday of Week 1.**
* **Buddy System:** New hire meets with their assigned "Onboarding Buddy" (a peer outside their direct reporting line) for coffee/chat.
* **Tool Access:** Manager ensures access to Jira, Salesforce, GitHub, or relevant department tools.

## Phase 4: The First 90 Days
* **30-Day Check-in:** HR conducts a survey to gauge onboarding satisfaction.
* **60-Day Check-in:** Manager reviews progress against initial goals.
* **90-Day Review:** Probationary period ends. Benefits fully vested (if applicable). Manager confirms "Graduation" from onboarding.

## I-9 Verification Requirements
Federal law requires physical inspection of documents within **3 business days** of the start date.
* **Remote Employees:** Must use an authorized notary or the "Authorized Representative" flow in Workday to verify documents remotely.
* **Office Employees:** Bring Passport or Driver's License + SSN card to the Office Manager.

## FAQ
**Q: My laptop hasn't arrived by Day 1. What do I do?**
A: Contact it-support@company.com immediately. You may join orientation via personal device, but do not download work files.

**Q: When do I get my first paycheck?**
A: We pay semi-monthly on the **15th and last day** of the month. If you start fewer than 5 days before a pay date, your first check will be included in the *following* cycle.
