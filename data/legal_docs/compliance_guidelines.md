# Regulatory Compliance Guidelines

**Policy Owner:** Chief Compliance Officer (CCO)
**Whistleblower Hotline:** 1-800-555-0123 (Anonymous)

## Anti-Bribery & Corruption (FCPA)
The company strictly prohibits offering, giving, soliciting, or accepting bribes.
* **Government Officials:** No gifts, meals, or entertainment may be provided to government officials (domestic or foreign) without prior written approval from Legal.
* **Commercial Gifts:**
    * **Giving:** Gifts to clients must be under **$100 in value**, infrequent, and branded (e.g., swag) where possible.
    * **Receiving:** Employees may not accept cash. Gifts over $100 must be disclosed to the CCO and may need to be returned or raffled to the team.

## Insider Trading Policy
If you possess Material Non-Public Information (MNPI), you may not trade company stock.
* **Trading Windows:** The trading window opens **2 full trading days** after the public release of quarterly earnings and closes **14 days** before the end of the next fiscal quarter.
* **Blackout Periods:** All Finance, Legal, and Exec Staff are automatically placed on blackout lists. You will be notified via email if this applies to you.

## Whistleblower Protection
We maintain a "Non-Retaliation" policy.
* **Reporting:** If you suspect financial fraud, harassment, or security violations, report it via the **EthicsPoint** portal or the hotline above.
* **Protection:** Retaliation against an employee for making a good-faith report is grounds for immediate termination of the retaliator.

## Conflicts of Interest
Employees must act in the best interest of the company.
* **Side Business:** You must disclose any side business that competes with or supplies the company.
* **Family Members:** You cannot hire or oversee a vendor owned by an immediate family member.
* **Disclosure Form:** All employees must update their Conflict of Interest disclosure annually in January via Workday.

## Antitrust & Competition
* **Competitors:** Never discuss pricing, territory allocation, or "agreements not to hire" with competitors.
* **Trade Associations:** If a competitor brings up pricing at a conference, leave the conversation immediately and document your departure.
